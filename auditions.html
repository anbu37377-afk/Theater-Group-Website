Audition Tips

Expert advice to help you shine during your audition

01

Prepare Your Material

Choose pieces that showcase your strengths and match the role you're auditioning for.

02

Arrive Early

Give yourself time to warm up, fill out paperwork, and get comfortable with the space.

03

Be Professional

Dress appropriately, be respectful to everyone, and show enthusiasm for the opportunity.

04

Stay Confident

Trust your preparation, take deep breaths, and remember that they want you to succeed.
