Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase tickets?

You can purchase tickets online through our website, by calling our box office, or by visiting us in person. Online tickets can be printed at home or shown on your mobile device.

What is your refund policy?

Refunds are available up to 24 hours before the performance. After that time, tickets are non-refundundable but may be exchanged for another performance.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! Groups of 10 or more receive a 15% discount, and groups of 20 or more receive a 25% discount. Contact our group sales department for more information.

Is the theater wheelchair accessible?

Yes, our theater is fully wheelchair accessible with designated seating areas and accessible restrooms. Please inform us when booking if you require accessible seating.

Can I audition for your productions?

Absolutely! We hold open auditions for most of our productions. Check our auditions page for upcoming opportunities and requirements.

Do you offer educational programs?

Yes, we offer workshops, masterclasses, and educational programs for all ages. Visit our education page for more information.
