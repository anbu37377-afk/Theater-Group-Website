Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Royal Theater Group

How do I purchase tickets?

You can purchase tickets online through our website, by calling our box office, or by visiting us in person during box office hours.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! We offer special rates for groups of 10 or more. Please contact our group sales department for more information and custom packages.

What is your refund policy?

Tickets are non-refundable but can be exchanged for another performance of the same show up to 24 hours before the scheduled performance time.
